Confetti!

Confetti is a variable font with a serif axis. In its sans serif form, Confetti is your ten AM workhorse. It’s your email over coffees, top button buttoned, sincerely and salutations, to the point productivity. Your ts are crossed and your Is are dotted. Consider it done.

In it’s serif form, Confetti is your three PM let’s wrap it up and ease into the evening with a team birthday event. It’s a walk outside, the top button unbuttoned, an afternoon snack break. It’s professional, but the evening is calling. You are taking liberties with your sign offs.
